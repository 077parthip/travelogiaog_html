Nohkalikai Falls

The tallest plunge waterfall in India, Nohkalikai Falls drops from a height of 340 meters. Surrounded by lush green hills, it's a must-visit natural wonder of Cherrapunji.

Living Root Bridges

These natural bridges are formed by training roots of rubber trees across streams. A unique blend of nature and human ingenuity, they are found in the nearby villages like Nongriat.

Mawsmai Cave

Seven Sisters Falls

Also known as Nohsngithiang Falls, this majestic waterfall cascades in seven segments from the cliff. It's especially breathtaking during the monsoon season.

Eco Park

Maintained by the Meghalaya government, Eco Park offers panoramic views of the Sylhet Plains of Bangladesh, flower gardens, and waterfalls originating right from the park.

❄️ Discover Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Solang Valley

Solang Valley is a winter wonderland popular for skiing, snowboarding, and snowmobiling. The snow-covered slopes and majestic views make it a favorite for adventure lovers.

Rohtang Pass

Rohtang Pass offers a breathtaking view of glaciers, peaks, and valleys. It’s often covered in snow in winter and is a hotspot for snow sports and scenic drives.

Old Manali

Old Manali is known for its peaceful ambiance, rustic cafes, and scenic beauty. Stroll through its snowy paths and experience the charm of Himalayan culture.

Hadimba Temple

Surrounded by cedar forests, Hadimba Temple is a historic wooden shrine dedicated to Goddess Hadimba. It looks magical when blanketed in snow during the winter.

Manu Temple

Dedicated to Sage Manu, this temple is a spiritual and peaceful site with a backdrop of snow-covered mountains and pine trees, perfect for winter visits.

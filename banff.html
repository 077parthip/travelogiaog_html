Lake Louise

Lake Louise is world-famous for its turquoise waters surrounded by snowy peaks. In winter, it becomes a frozen wonderland perfect for ice skating and snowy hikes.

Banff Gondola

Take the Banff Gondola up Sulphur Mountain to enjoy panoramic views of the snow-covered Canadian Rockies. At the summit, explore boardwalks and a modern interpretive center.

Johnston Canyon

In winter, Johnston Canyon becomes a magical trail with frozen waterfalls and icy paths. Perfect for a guided ice walk adventure through the snowy forest.

Banff Upper Hot Springs

Relax in warm mineral-rich waters while surrounded by snowy landscapes. The Banff Upper Hot Springs are a perfect way to unwind after a day in the cold.

Downtown Banff

Downtown Banff is full of charming shops, cafes, and alpine-style restaurants. The snow-covered streets are beautifully lit during the winter months, creating a cozy village feel.

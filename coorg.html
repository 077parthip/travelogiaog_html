Abbey Falls

Abbey Falls is a scenic waterfall nestled amidst coffee plantations. The water cascades down from a height of 70 feet, creating a mesmerizing view, especially during the monsoon season.

Raja's Seat

Raja's Seat offers a panoramic view of the mist-covered hills and valleys of Coorg. This scenic garden was a favorite spot of Kodagu kings and is now a perfect place for sunsets and relaxation.

Dubare Elephant Camp

Talacauvery

Talacauvery is the origin of the sacred River Cauvery, nestled in the Brahmagiri hills. It is also a popular pilgrimage site, surrounded by serene landscapes and spiritual ambiance.

Namdroling Monastery

Also known as the Golden Temple, Namdroling Monastery is a stunning Tibetan Buddhist monastery with golden roofs, intricate artwork, and a peaceful atmosphere.
